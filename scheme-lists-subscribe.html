Schemedoc and Schemeweb

There are two SRFI mailing lists that are being used to discuss ideas that may or may not eventually lead to SRFIs:

Schemedoc (archive)
collecting, organizing, and serving indexes of Scheme code found in SRFIs, Scheme implementations, RnRS standards, etc.
Schemeweb (archive)
web technology implemented in Scheme, e.g. web servers and web request handlers

Both mailing lists are open to all to join.

Neither of these mailing lists is included in the srfi-auto-subscribe mailing list, which includes all the other SRFI mailing lists.

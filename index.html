Scheme Requests for Implementation

The "Scheme Requests for Implementation" (SRFI) process is a new approach to helping Scheme users to write portable and yet useful code. It is a forum for people interested in coordinating libraries and other additions to the Scheme language between implementations.

Attention SRFI Authors: the SRFI editors are trying to contact the authors of SRFI 5, Andy Gaynor; 24, Antti Huima; and 49, Egil Möller concerning a licensing change for SRFI documents. Please send email to srfi-editors@srfi.schemers.org if you are one of these authors, or know how to contact any of them.

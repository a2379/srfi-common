Scheme Requests for Implementation

The "Scheme Requests for Implementation" (SRFI) process is a new approach to helping Scheme users to write portable and yet useful code. It is a forum for people interested in coordinating libraries and other additions to the Scheme language between implementations.

Attention SRFI Authors: the SRFI editors are trying to contact the authors of SRFI 5, Andy Gaynor and 24, Antti Huima concerning a licensing change for SRFI documents. Please send email to srfi minus editors at srfi dot schemers dot org if you are one of these authors, or know how to contact any of them.
